About Us

Choosing an ideal car is a mess for almost every Indian consumer. Most people can't decide among which vehicle to choose from the vast portfolio made available by the Indian manufacturers.

With ShowMyRide, our intention was to replace the age-old tradition of comparing cars blindly based on just numbers and features without studying what exactly the consumer is looking for in a vehicle. Comparisons does not do any recommendation but rather just displays the the specifications that the manufacturer of the vehicle publishes. In this fast-paced world, most consumers don’t have any idea how the performance of a vehicle can be calculated with just comparing the power and torque figures. That’s why with ShowMyRide, using ML tools to recommend a vehicle will allow a customer get an ideal suggestion based on exactly what he is looking for in a vehicle.
